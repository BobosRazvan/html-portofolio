About Me

Early Years:

Razvan Bobos, born and raised in the bustling city of Silicon Valley, always had an insatiable appetite for technology and a passion for the game of basketball. From a young age, John demonstrated his curiosity for computers by dismantling and reassembling family gadgets, much to his parents' chagrin. It was clear that he was destined for a future in computer science.

Academic Journey:

As Razvan progressed through his education, his love for computer science grew exponentially. He was the go-to person for fixing classmates' computers, and his coding skills were nothing short of impressive. He pursued a degree in Computer Science at the prestigious Silicon University, where he excelled both academically and technologically. Professors often marveled at his ability to turn complex algorithms into elegant solutions.

On the court:

But Razvan's talents didn't stop at the keyboard. When he wasn't immersed in the world of coding, he was dominating on the basketball court. Standing at a towering 6'5", he was a force to be reckoned with. His precision in programming mirrored his finesse on the court, where he was known for his deadly three-pointers and jaw-dropping dunks. His teammates fondly nicknamed him "Tech Hoops."
